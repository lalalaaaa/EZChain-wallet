The Vision

The development of underlying technologies in blockchain mostly revolves around a difficult problem: how to enhance the performance of the system and reduce various costs of nodes (such as communication, storage and verification) without compromising the system's security and decentralization.

Ezchain proposes a novel solution that addresses these challenges through innovative scale-out architecture, enabling blockchain technology to truly support ledger applications in large-scale fully decentralized networks.

Research Paper

A Scale-out Decentralized Blockchain Ledger System for Web3.0

Lide Xue, Wei Yang, Wei Li

This paper proposes EZchain---a novel decentralized "scale-out" ledger system designed for web3.0, aiming to enable blockchain technology to truly support ledger applications in large-scale fully decentralized networks. Without compromising security and decentralization, EZchain successfully accomplishes significant milestones in scalability, hardware compatibility, and transaction efficiency.

Key Achievements

Performance Breakthrough

Our prototype experiment demonstrates that under typical daily bandwidth network conditions, EZchain's performance in all aspects approaches that of the accounts in centralized payment systems.

Mobile Payment Infrastructure

Provides a solid infrastructure for realizing mobile payments in web3.0, bridging the gap between blockchain technology and everyday financial applications.

Large-scale Networks

Successfully addresses the challenges of large-scale fully decentralized networks, enabling blockchain technology to support truly global applications.
