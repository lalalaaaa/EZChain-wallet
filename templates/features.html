Infinite Scalability

The theoretical throughput of EZchain can be infinitely expanded, nearly unaffected by bandwidth and other resource constraints. This makes it ideal for large-scale decentralized networks.

Consumer-Grade Hardware

EZchain is designed to be compatible with consumer-grade hardware, supporting storage, computation, and verification requirements without expensive specialized equipment.

Fast Transaction Confirmation

EZchain strives to maintain transaction confirmation delays within one minute, providing a user experience similar to centralized payment systems.

True Decentralization

Maintains strict adherence to decentralization principles while ensuring robust security through innovative consensus mechanisms.

Scale-out Architecture

Novel "scale-out" ledger system designed specifically for Web3.0, enabling blockchain technology to support large-scale applications.

Mobile Payment Ready

Prototype experiments show performance approaching centralized payment systems, providing solid infrastructure for mobile payments in Web3.0.
