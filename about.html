About Ezchain

Our Mission

Ezchain was born from the vision of creating a truly scalable blockchain system that could support the growing demands of Web3.0 without compromising on decentralization or security. We believe that blockchain technology should be accessible, efficient, and capable of handling real-world applications at scale.

Our mission is to revolutionize the blockchain landscape by introducing innovative architectural solutions that break free from the limitations of traditional systems. Through our research and development, we're building the foundation for the next generation of decentralized applications.

Research Foundation

Authors: Ruiheng Xu, Hongyang Li, Zhipeng Wang, Yuguang Fang

Title: "Ezchain: A Scale-out Blockchain Ledger System in Fully Decentralized Networks"

Published: arXiv:2312.00281 [cs.CR] - December 2023

Abstract

Blockchain technology is crucial for Web 3.0, but existing systems face significant scalability challenges. In this paper, we propose Ezchain, a novel scale-out blockchain ledger system designed for fully decentralized networks. Unlike traditional blockchains where system throughput is constrained by bandwidth resources, Ezchain's throughput is directly proportional to the node size, enabling infinite scalability. Our system maintains strict adherence to decentralization principles while achieving efficient transaction confirmation within one minute. Through extensive experiments, we demonstrate that Ezchain can support consumer-grade hardware while maintaining robust security and performance characteristics.
